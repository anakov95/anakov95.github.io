About

I am a freelance web developer and photographer based in Naples, FL.

I specialize in front end web development, graphic design, portrait and landscape photography and photo editing and manipulation. 

 

My very first web design project was the website for the school I was going to. It was a collaboration between a few students. Since then I have helped a few people design websites for their businesses. Recently I stared learning Python and I am working on a web application that will suit my photography needs.
